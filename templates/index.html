Annotator Instructions

Please review these instructions before starting.

Before You Start

  1. Use headphones for consistent audio quality.
  2. Work in a quiet environment.
  3. Take breaks every 30-60 minutes to maintain accuracy.

How to Annotate

  1. Click Play to listen to each audio clip.
  2. You can replay as many times as needed.
  3. Rate your emotional perception on three scales: Valence, Arousal, Dominance.
  4. Select the figure/number from 1-9 that best matches your perception.
  5. Submit to move to the next clip.

Important Clip Context

  • Many clips are truncated segments from longer interactions.
  • Some clips may include fuller context from reconstructed audio.
  • Focus on the speaker's emotional state in the clip you hear.
  • Use your interpretation consistently across all clips.

Tips

  • Trust your first impression.
  • Focus on emotion rather than spoken content.
  • Stay consistent in your ratings across clips.
